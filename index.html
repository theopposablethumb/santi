Project 1: Concept Art for Animated Short Film "Head Room".

For the animated short film "Head Room," I worked as the concept artist. My role focused on developing unique characters that seamlessly fit the story.

I focussed on creating through multiple iterations iconic silhouettes and morphologies that represented each character's personality, emphasizing the core principles of character design.

Lastly, I developed expression sheets to capture the most characteristic emotions of each character, the key poses to show how they interact with their environment. and created turnarounds to ensure consistency from all angles.
